LABS

CSE has modern laboratories to serve the teaching and research needs of the students and faculty. All systems are connected to the Gigabit LAN, which is a part of the Institute-wide area network.

The Central Computing Facility (CCF) of the institute is also supervised by CSE faculty. Including facility in CCF, CSE has around 350 Desktops that cater to the needs of UG/PG/PhD students.

Internet connectivity available in the institute is of 64 Mbps leased line from Reliance. CSE owns 5 Xeon Servers (3 GHz) and around 350 Desktop PCs. The PG laboratory is equipped with recent i7 Processor PCs to support heavy computing needs.

Recently, CSE has signed an MoU with DLINK. DLINK has supplied networking equipment to set up the laboratory and run certification courses.
